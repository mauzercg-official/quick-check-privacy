Privacy Policy for Quick Check

Last Updated: December 28, 2024

Data Collection

Quick Check does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All camera and microphone processing happens locally in your browser.

Permissions

The extension requests camera and microphone access solely to display your video preview and audio levels. This data never leaves your device.

Storage

The extension only stores your device preferences (selected camera/microphone) locally using Chrome's storage API. This data is not transmitted anywhere.

Third Parties

Quick Check does not share any information with third parties. No analytics, no tracking, no data collection of any kind.
